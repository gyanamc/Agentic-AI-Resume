Everything — inference, retrieval, fine-tuning, evaluation — runs on SBI Card's own infrastructure.

The platform integrates 45+ enterprise components:
- Orchestration layer: LangGraph for stateful multi-agent coordination and LangChain for pipeline management

- Evaluation layer: Ragas (faithfulness 0.90, relevancy 0.94) + MLflow tracking + Prometheus/Grafana infrastructure observability
